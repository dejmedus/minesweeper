Help

Minesweeper rules:

  • Left click to search a square.
  • Numbers express how many mines are adjacent to the square.
  • Right click to place a flag - this prevents potential mines from being clicked.
  • Click on a mine and you lose.
  • Discover every non-mine square to win.
